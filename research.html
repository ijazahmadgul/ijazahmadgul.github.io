Digital Financial Inclusion and Economic Growth in Emerging Markets: A Panel Data Analysis (with Sarah Khan & Muhammad Ali), 2023, Journal of Development Economics

Abstract

This study examines the relationship between digital financial inclusion and economic growth across 15 emerging market economies from 2010-2020. Using panel data analysis and instrumental variable techniques, we find that a 1% increase in digital financial inclusion is associated with a 0.3% increase in GDP per capita growth. The results are robust across different specifications and highlight the importance of mobile banking and digital payment systems in promoting economic development.

Labor Market Dynamics and Youth Employment in South Asia: Evidence from Pakistan (with Noman Shah), 2023, Applied Economics Letters

Abstract

Youth unemployment remains a persistent challenge in Pakistan, with rates exceeding 25% among the 15-24 age group. This paper analyzes labor market dynamics using data from the Pakistan Labour Force Survey (2015-2020) and identifies key barriers to youth employment including skills mismatch, limited job creation in formal sectors, and regional disparities. Our findings suggest that targeted skills development programs and entrepreneurship support could significantly improve youth employment outcomes.
