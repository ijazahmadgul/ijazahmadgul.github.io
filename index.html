Welcome to My Academic Homepage!

I hold a PhD in Higher Education Administration from the University of Georgia, Athens. My supervisor was Professor James C Hearn.

I also hold a Master's in Research & Innovation in Higher Education (MARIHE) from Tampere University (Finland). This program was jointly offered by Tampere University (Finland), Danube University Krems (Austria), Osnabrück University of Applied Sciences (Germany), and Beijing Normal University (China), and was sponsored by the European Erasmus Mundus Scholarship Program.

Research Fields: Policy Analysis, Advocacy, Institutional Change, Higher Education Funding, Governance, Quality Assurance, Policy Diffusion
